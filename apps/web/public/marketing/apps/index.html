Get SlyTab on your phone

The web app at electricrv.ca/slytab works everywhere — these are the ways to make it feel like a native app.

📱 Add to your home screen (works today)

The full app — receipt scanning included — runs in your browser and installs like an app:

  1. Android (Chrome): open the app → tap the ⋮ menu → Add to Home screen.
  2. iPhone (Safari): open the app → tap the Share icon → Add to Home Screen.

🤖 Android app private beta

The native Android app is in family testing. Ask Dave for the current APK — it installs directly (you may need to allow "install unknown apps" for your browser).

🍎 iPhone app coming soon

The iOS app is headed for TestFlight. When it's ready, an invite link will appear here and in the app.
