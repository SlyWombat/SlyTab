SlyTab Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026 · Data controller: Electric RV, Ontario, Canada

SlyTab is a private expense-sharing app made for families and friends. Our privacy position is simple: your data is yours, it stays on our server in Canada, and we never sell it, share it for advertising, or run analytics on it.

What we collect

  • Account: your email address, display name, and a hash of your password (we can never see the password itself).
  • Expense data: the groups you're in, expenses, splits, settlements, and any notes you enter — visible only to members of the same group.
  • Payment handles (optional): an Interac e-Transfer email, PayPal.Me, or Venmo username you choose to save so group members can pay you. SlyTab never processes, holds, or has access to money or banking credentials.
  • Receipt photos (optional): stored with the expense, visible only to the group.

What we don't collect

No analytics, no telemetry, no advertising or tracking SDKs, no location data, no contact-list access, no third-party sign-in.

Third parties we use

  • Receipt scanning runs on our own hardware. Photos are itemized by a vision model we host ourselves — they are never sent to any third-party AI service. (A Claude API fallback exists in the code but is disabled unless we explicitly enable it; this policy will be updated first if that ever changes.)
  • Frankfurter/ECB: currency codes only, to fetch daily exchange rates. No user data is sent.

Where your data lives

On our server hosted for electricrv.ca, with encrypted transport (HTTPS), hashed credentials, and nightly backups retained for 30 days.

Your rights

Under PIPEDA you may access, export, correct, or delete your personal information at any time. Export is built in (Profile → Export). Deleting your account removes your personal data; your share of historical group expenses is anonymized so other members' balances stay correct.
