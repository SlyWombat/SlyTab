SlySplit — Brand & UI

The "Ledger" design language applied: the split-coin identity, the token palette, and five high-fidelity screens from the v1.0 flow. Everything below is built from the tokens in docs/design/DESIGN.md — what ships is what you see.

Identity

The split coin

A coin divided by an S-shaped seam — the S of SlySplit. One half brand blue, one half owed-green: money, split fairly, between two parties.

Palette

Ledger tokens

Direction has exactly two colours — green means owed to you, amber means you owe. Red is reserved for errors, so money-direction and danger never blur.
